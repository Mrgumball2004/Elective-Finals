About Me

personal details and background information.


          Welcome to our adventure! As artists who're passionate, about both technology and 
          art we are excited to pursue our studies in the IT field at NBSC.

We believe that the fusion of creativity and technology brings joy and inspiration.

With a combination of talent and technical expertise we approach our work as 
aspiring web developers with enthusiasm.
    
    Our shared love for the arts is the driving force behind our efforts motivating us 
      to create captivating and technically robust digital experiences. 

    As students at NBSC we fully immerse ourselves in the world of web 
development constantly refining our skills to transform our visions into captivating 
platforms.

    Our ultimate goal is to blend art with technology establishing ourselves as 
creators in the digital realm.
    
    Join us on this journey where pixels harmonize with passion and where art 
converges with code.

    Together as artists and IT enthusiasts we are committed, to pushing boundaries 
 and shaping the future of expression.
